This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your personal information when you opt in to receive SMS text messages from this portfolio. By opting in, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

When you opt in to SMS notifications, I collect the following information:

  • Full name

  • Opt-in consent date and method

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Sending you SMS notifications related to portfolio updates and QA testing opportunities
  • Responding to your SMS replies

4. Data Security & Protection

I take your data security seriously. Your personal information is:

  • Stored securely through Twilio's compliant infrastructure

  • Accessible only to authorized personnel managing SMS communications

5. Third-Party Services

Your SMS communications are processed through Twilio, a third-party SMS service provider. Twilio may process your data according to their privacy policies, but I do not share your opt-in data with any other third parties.

6. Your Rights & Opt-Out

You have the right to:

7. Message Frequency & Charges

SMS frequency varies based on portfolio updates and QA testing opportunities. Standard message and data rates from your cellular provider may apply. I do not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

8. Children's Privacy

This SMS service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. I do not knowingly collect information from minors. If I become aware of such collection, I will delete the information immediately.
